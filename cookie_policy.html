Cookie Policy

Last Updated:

This Cookie Policy explains how domain (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at domain.com. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is being used.

Cookies can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (remain on your device for a defined period or until manually deleted). They can also be first-party cookies (set by us) or third-party cookies (set by external services we use).

Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies used on our website into the following types:

Essential

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the site cannot function without them.

  • Session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing
  • Cookie consent preferences

Retention: Session or up to 1 year

Analytics

Analytical Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This allows us to improve our website and services.

  • Page view tracking
  • User journey analysis
  • Traffic source identification
  • Bounce rate measurement

Retention: Up to 24 months

Marketing

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display ads that are relevant and engaging. They may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners.

  • Behavioral advertising
  • Retargeting campaigns
  • Ad performance measurement
  • Social media integration

Retention: Up to 90 days

Functional

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

  • Language preferences
  • User interface customization
  • Chat and support widgets
  • Embedded media playback

Retention: Up to 12 months

Purposes of Cookie Use

We use cookies for the following specific purposes:

  • Authentication & Security: To recognize you when you log in and protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • Performance Monitoring: To measure site speed, server response times, and identify technical issues affecting user experience.
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences such as language, region, and accessibility settings.
  • Analytics & Insights: To understand which pages and features are most popular, and how users navigate our site.
  • Advertising & Remarketing: To deliver targeted advertisements based on your interests and browsing behavior.
  • Communication: To enable live chat support and customer feedback features.
  • Compliance: To record your cookie consent decisions in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Cookie Retention Periods

Different cookies remain on your device for different periods of time:

Session Cookies

Deleted automatically when you close your browser. No data persists after your session ends.

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a defined period (ranging from 30 days up to 24 months) or until you delete them manually.

Cookie Category Typical Retention Period
Necessary / Essential Session – 12 months
Analytical / Performance Up to 24 months
Marketing / Advertising Up to 90 days
Functional / Preference Up to 12 months
Consent Record Up to 12 months

How to Manage Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing and controlling cookies. Please note that restricting cookies may impact the functionality of certain parts of our website.

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences by category. You can revisit and update your choices at any time.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View which cookies have been set on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is set

Browser-specific guidance is available here:

  • Google Chrome Cookie Settings
  • Mozilla Firefox Cookie Settings
  • Apple Safari Cookie Settings
  • Microsoft Edge Cookie Settings

Opt-Out Tools

You can also use the following opt-out tools provided by third parties:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out — Browser add-on to prevent Google Analytics tracking.
  • Meta Ad Preferences — Manage your Facebook and Instagram ad settings.
  • Your Online Choices — EU-based opt-out platform for behavioral advertising.
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out — US-based opt-out tool for interest-based ads.

Your Consent

In accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive, we require your explicit consent before placing any non-essential cookies on your device.

When you first visit our website, our cookie consent tool will present you with clear options to accept or decline each category of cookies. Your consent is:

  • Freely given: You are never required to consent to non-essential cookies.
  • Specific: You can accept or reject each cookie category individually.
  • Informed: We explain clearly what each cookie type does before you consent.
  • Unambiguous: Consent is obtained through a clear affirmative action (not pre-ticked boxes).
  • Withdrawable: You can withdraw your consent at any time through our cookie preference center.

Your consent preferences are stored as a cookie for up to 12 months. After this period, we will ask for your consent again.

GDPR & ePrivacy Compliance

Our cookie practices comply with the following applicable laws and regulations:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — EU 2016/679: Governs the processing of personal data including data collected via cookies from EU/EEA residents.
  • ePrivacy Directive — 2002/58/EC (Cookie Law): Requires informed consent before non-essential cookies are placed on a user’s device.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA): Provides California residents with rights regarding their personal data, including data collected via cookies.

For information about how we handle your personal data more broadly, please see our Privacy Policy. To exercise your data rights under GDPR, please visit our GDPR Rights page.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Cookie Policy.
