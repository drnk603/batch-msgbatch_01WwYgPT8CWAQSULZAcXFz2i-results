Our Instructors

Meet the People Behind PilatesFlow

Our certified instructors bring years of professional experience, genuine warmth, and an unwavering commitment to your wellness journey. Every session is guided by expertise and heart.

Team Members

Sarah Mitchell

Lead Pilates Instructor & Co-Founder

With over 14 years of teaching classical and contemporary Pilates, Sarah has transformed the studio into a sanctuary for mindful movement. A certified BASI Pilates instructor, she specializes in postural correction and injury rehabilitation.

  • BASI Pilates Certified
  • Rehabilitation Specialist

James Okafor

Men's Pilates & Athletic Conditioning Coach

A former professional athlete turned certified Pilates instructor, James brings a performance-based approach to studio training. He helps athletes and active adults improve core stability, flexibility, and injury resilience through targeted Pilates programming.

  • Polestar Pilates Certified
  • NSCA Certified Strength Coach

Elena Vasquez

Prenatal & Postnatal Pilates Specialist

Elena is passionate about supporting women through every stage of motherhood. With specialized certifications in prenatal and postnatal fitness, she creates safe, effective programs that nurture both physical and emotional wellbeing during pregnancy and recovery.

  • Stott Pilates Certified
  • Prenatal Fitness Specialist

Marcus Lee

Mind-Body Wellness Coach

Marcus integrates classical Pilates with mindfulness practices to create a holistic approach to wellness. Drawing from a background in psychology and somatic movement therapy, he helps clients reconnect with their bodies and build lasting healthy habits.

  • Power Pilates Certified
  • Somatic Movement Therapist

Priya Sharma

Senior Pilates Instructor

Priya brings a deeply classical approach to Pilates, having trained directly under second-generation instructors. Her sessions are known for their precision, depth, and transformative quality. She runs our advanced Reformer and Cadillac programs.

  • Classical Pilates Certified
  • Advanced Apparatus Specialist

Olivia Grant

Studio Manager & Client Experience Lead

Olivia ensures every client who walks through our doors feels welcomed, supported, and inspired. With a background in hospitality management and a personal love for Pilates, she bridges the gap between exceptional customer care and a thriving studio culture.

  • Studio Operations Certified
  • Wellness Hospitality Specialist
